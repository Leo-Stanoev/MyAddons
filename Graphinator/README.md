# Graphinator

A World of Warcraft addon that visualizes Auctionator historical price data using a dynamic, interactive bar graph. Track market trends at a glance directly within the Auction House or via bag hovers.

## Features
* **Visual Price History:** Plots historical minimum prices over a customizable timeframe (up to 60 days).
* **Auction House Integration:** Automatically updates the graph when you browse items or prepare to buy/sell auctions.
* **Bag Hover Updates:** Optional feature to update the history panel simply by hovering over items in your inventory.
* **Market Value Line:** Displays a blue horizontal reference line representing the current market value against historical data.
* **Interactive Bars:** Hover over specific bars for exact date and price data, or click a bar to instantly search for that item on the Auction House.
* **Customizable UI:** Resizable panel with configurable price labels, date formats, and chronological time-gap displays.

## Requirements
* **Auctionator:** This addon requires Auctionator to be installed and its price database loaded to function.

## Usage
* **Toggle Panel:** Click the small `<` or `>` tab attached to the side of the Auction House window to show or hide the graph.
* **Settings:** Click the cogwheel icon at the top right of the history panel to open the settings popup.
* **Configuration:** From the popup, you can toggle price/date labels, change the number of days displayed, and enable "Update on bag hover".
* **Blizzard Options:** Enable or disable the core addon functionality via the standard Interface > AddOns menu.
