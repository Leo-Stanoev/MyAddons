# TradeSkillProfits

A World of Warcraft (TBC Classic) addon that calculates and displays Auction House profit margins directly within your crafting window. Stop guessing which crafts make money and start maximizing your gold per hour.

## Features
* **Live Profit Calculations:** Instantly displays the estimated profit or loss next to every recipe.
* **Advanced Sorting:** Sort your recipe list by highest profit or lowest crafting cost.
* **Favorites & Filtering:** Right-click to favorite your best recipes, or Shift-Right-Click to banish unprofitable crafts to a filtered list at the bottom.
* **Profession Tabs:** Quick-switch between your other crafting professions directly from the TradeSkill window.
* **Tooltip Breakdown:** Adds a clean breakdown of Crafting Cost, AH Value, and Total Profit directly into item tooltips.

## Requirements
* **Auctionator:** This addon relies on Auctionator's pricing database to calculate material costs and final item values.

## Usage
* Open your TradeSkill window and use the new **Sort Button** (Default/Profit/Cost).
* **Middle-Click** the sort button to toggle between Profit and Cost sorting.
* **Right-Click** the sort button to toggle Ascending/Descending order.
* Type `/tsp` in chat to open the options panel to customize visuals, coin icons, and tooltip settings.
